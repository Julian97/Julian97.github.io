My Story

My Story

I have been doing freelance photography for the past 6 years and I specialise in helping companies, startups, societies and individuals expand their outreach with high-quality shots of their events or subjects.

I know my own gear intimately well to the point where I can shoot effortlessly in complete darkness so you don't have to worry about precious time lost on the ground fumbling with equipment and camera settings.

In the last year alone I've provided dozens of clients with high-quality shots of many different genres such as portraiture, event coverage, product shoots, graduation, fashion, architectural, astrophotos, solar and lunar eclipses etc. I have been able to help them engage and one client even grew their viewership by up to 1000%.

I have a keen eye and sharp attention to details. I pride myself in creating unique, engaging and high quality content that I know the audience loves and wants to share with their peers.

  • Natural and candid photos
  • No excessive editing and filters
  • 24 megapixel photos, 6000*4000 pixel size

What I'm looking for is something that I can add value to and provide a positive ROI. Is this what you are looking for?
